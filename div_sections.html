Documents Verification

  1. When Police comes to the user he will enter the vehicle number in our system by logging with essential credentials.

  2. Then the system will provide the respective vehicle document details

  3. The result includes the dates of the vehicle document.

  4. If the user doesn`t renewal his/her document then the police will make penalty

Sending Reminders

  1. Sending Email notifications to the vehicle owner before 30 days / 15 days / 7 days / 1 day to the expiring date.

  2. So that the vehicle owner can renewal his/her documents within the specified time.

  3. If the vehicle owner doesn`t update the documents and doesn`t use the vehicle till days then that particular vehicle details will be deleted from the database permanently.
